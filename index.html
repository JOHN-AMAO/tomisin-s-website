Wikipedia

Wikipedia, a free online encyclopedia, is a global collaborative project that empowers individuals to share knowledge. Created in 2001, it has grown to become one of the most comprehensive sources of information, available in over 300 languages. Anyone with internet access can edit and contribute to its articles, fostering a diverse range of perspectives. Though not without its controversies, Wikipedia remains a vital tool for research, learning, and accessing a vast array of topics across countless disciplines. Its open-source nature exemplifies the potential of collective intelligence in the digital age.
